== Description ==

Join The Celebration & Decorate Your Wordpress.

Choose from 200+ hand drawn decorations.
Keep your website live and show everyone what's important for you.

**Visit our [demo page](http://decorationsforoccasions.com) to see how cool they actually are ;)**


== Installation ==

1. Install ZIP file or upload plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/perfect-decorations-for-occasions` directory.
2. Activate Decorations For Occasions through the Plugins menu in WordPress.
3. When prompted, install the *Perfect Core Library*. Don't worry, it's automatic.
4. Select *Decorations For Occasions* in the backend menu and start your trial.
5. Celebrate occasions that matter to you

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= 1. Is this plugin free? =

After the two week long trial period expires, you will always have access to 26 decorations in 2 free channels. To get access to hundreds of amazing decorations you will have to purchase them, however you can also extend your free trial by referring our plugin to other people - if they enter your e-mail address while creating their free trial, you will be granted additional 5 days of free trial!

= 2. How to enable Decorations for Occasions on your site? =

After installing our plugin, you can enable and disable various decorations on *Occasions List* and *Occasions Timeline*. Additionally you can toggle automatic decoration display via setting in *Option* tab (*Should upcoming events be displayed automatically*).

= 3. Compatibility =

Our plugin requires WordPress version 3.7+ and PHP version 5.3+.
